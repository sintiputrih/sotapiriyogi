  • Heat oven to 400 degrees.

  • On floured workspace, roll out dough into rectangle, approximately 14 x 14 inches.

  • In small bowl, mix melted butter and garlic powder together.

  • Spread all over pizza dough.

  • Top with parmesan and mozzarella cheeses.

  • Starting at the long end nearest you, begin rolling up the dough.

  • Use a sharp knife to slice into 2 inch rolls.

  • Transfer the rolls onto greased baking sheet.

  • Tighten up the rolls as needed. Optional -- brush with egg wash to "glue" the ends together.

  • Bake for 13-16 minutes or until lightly golden brown and cheese is melted.
