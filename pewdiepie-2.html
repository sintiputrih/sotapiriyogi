PewDiePie aka Felix Arvid Ulf Kjellberg is a Swedish YouTuber comedian and social media personality. As of 2023, PewDiePie’s net worth is $53 million. He is mainly known for his Let’s Play videos and other comedy and fun videos on his YouTube channel.

His YouTube channel is the second most subscribed YouTube Channel all over the world after T-Series. He is also listed among the world’s 100 most influential people by Time magazine in the year 2016.

PewDiePie Wiki/Biography

Born on 24 October 1989, PewDiePie’s age is 33 Years Old as of 2023. He was born and brought up in a well-settled family from Gothenburg, Sweden. Currently, he is living in Brighton, England, the United Kingdom with his family. He is known to hold a Swedish nationality.

Career

PewDiePie started his career as a YouTuber and started a self-titled YouTube channel on which he started uploading Let’s Play videos of horror and action video games. Initially, he worked as a harbor captain, selling prints of his Photoshop art, and working at a dog stand to support himself financially as his family denied supporting him economically.

Before that, he started learning Adobe Photoshop and participated in many photoshop contests during his graduation period at Chalmers and almost earned an apprenticeship at a Scandinavian advertising agency but he left Chalmers to focus on his online career as a YouTuber.

Initially, he focused on videos related to video game commentaries, especially horror and action video games including Minecraft and Call of Duty. He gained popularity for his Let’s Play videos of Amnesia The Dark Descent and other related videos. In the year 2011, he started uploading vlogs weekly under the name Fridays with PewDiePie. In the year 2012, he signed a contract with Maker Studios that helped in the growth of his YouTube channel.

He also used to make videos in which he makes jokes on rape earlier in his YouTube career but soon he realized his mistake as he started facing criticism. In the year 2012, he posted a post on Tumblr in which he wrote an apology to everyone stating that “he never wants to hurt anyone and he apologizes whatever he did”. His oldest video titled Minecraft Multiplayer Fun has over 14 million views as of May 2020.

In the year 2013, PewDiePie gained recognition all over the world for his amazing videos surpassing 6 million subscribers on his YouTube channel and he also won the Swedish Social Star at the inaugural Starcount Social Stars Awards in Singapore. He also becomes the second-most followed YouTuber and received a certificate from Guinness World Records for his achievement.

In the year 2014, Maker Studios released an official PewDiePie application for iPhone users which allows users to watch his videos, create custom feeds, and share PewDiePie videos with other users as well. In the same year, he also co-hosted a series titled Broken along with popular video games commentator Cinnamon ToastKen or Kenneth Morrison.

In the year 2016 and 2017, PewDiePie has released a string of videos on his channel after YouTube has changed its algorithms. During the year 2015, it was seen that he has changed his style, he continued uploading video game videos but apart from that, he started taking risks and uploading videos related to viral challenges, the fun of other YouTubers, and other content videos.

In the year 2015, he has also been featured in the popular YouTube Red series titled Scare PewDiePie. In the year 2017, he created a crowdsourced channel on the live streaming platform named Twitch and started a series titled Best Club.

In the year 2018, PewDiePie uploaded a video against Indian company T-Series titled T-SERIES DISS TRACK, and later in the same year, he uploaded an episode on his channel in which he mentioned about 2019 Pulwama terrorist attack. Soon T-Series surpass PewDiePie and become the most-subscribed channel on YouTube.

Along with that he also endorses several brands and promotes them on his channel. Recently, he also launched Gaming Week and after several years he only focused on his Lets’s Play videos especially for Minecraft games.

Awards

PewDiePie has won many popular awards throughout his online career. He has won the Starcount Social Star Awards for Most Popular Social Show in the year 2013. He has also won the 5th Shorty Awards and Teen Choice Awards in the year 2014.

Controversy

PewDiePie frequently landed himself into controversies as some of his videos appeared to be a racial slur for everyone. He faced criticism for his racial discrimination related videos and some of his videos even got blocked by YouTube.

He also had a conflict with Bob Iger who was the then-CEO of the Walt Disney Company and even lost his contract with Maker Studios which is also a subsidiary of the Disney company. The reason for the conflict between them was that PewDiePie had uploaded a video in which he read out erotic fan fiction about Disney characters in the film titled Frozen.

After that, he also had a conflict with India’s most popular music studio T-Series as he made a fun video of the T-Series on his channel. After which T-Series company also filed a complaint against him in the Delhi High Court which later issued an order that PewDiePie videos have been blocked in India. As per reports, two parties had settled the matter in July 2019.
