Anne V is A-ok! Anne Vyalitsnya recently opened up about ex Adam Levine during an appearance on E!’s Fashion Police, and had nothing but nice things to say about his future bride Behati Prinsloo.

“Who cares? I’m such a believer in love,” the 27-year-old replied when asked about the couple’s engagement. “I will love him forever. He is such a wonderful guy. But we didn’t work out.”

The Russian-born model and Levine, 34, split back in April 2012 after two years of dating. “Adam and I have decided to separate in an amicable and supportive manner,” she told Us in a statement. “We still love and respect each other as friends. I wish him all the best.” (An insider told Us Levine was “blindsided” by the breakup at the time.)
