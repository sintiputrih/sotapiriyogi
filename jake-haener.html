Early Life

High School Career

He went to school in Danville, California, at Monte Vista. Haener threw for 8,464 yards and a school-record 90 touchdowns throughout his high school career. He committed to play football at the University of Washington during his undergraduate career.

College Career

After redshirting his freshman year at Washington in 2017, Haener saw action in four games as Jake Browning’s backup in 2018. He completed nine of 13 passes for 107 yards, one touchdown, and one interception.

In 2019, Haener transferred to California State University, Fresno. Haener didn’t play in his first season at Fresno State due to transfer requirements, but he did start in 2020. He completed 150 of 232 passes for 2,021 yards, 14 touchdowns, and five interceptions over the course of six games. In 2021, he remained the starter.

Professional Career
