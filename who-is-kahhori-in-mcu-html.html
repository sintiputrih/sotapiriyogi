Who is Kahhori in MCU?

September 2024 · 2 minute read
Spread the love             

So, who exactly is Kahhori? | Well, she’s not your typical superhero with a comic book background; in fact, she’s the first of her kind in the MCU. Voiced by the talented Canadian Mohawk actress, Devery Jacobs, Kahhori is a young Mohawk woman from the Haudenosaunee Confederacy in pre-colonial America. Now, that’s something new and refreshing, right?

Why Kahhori Matters in the MCU

Now, you might be wondering why Kahhori’s debut is such a big deal. Well, for starters, she brings much-needed Indigenous representation to the MCU. It’s like opening a new chapter in the Marvel universe, offering us a fresh perspective on history through the eyes of the land’s first peoples.

The Star-Studded “What If…? Season 2” Cast

Let’s take a moment to appreciate the fantastic cast behind “What If… Kahhori Reshaped the World.” Devery Jacobs voices our Mohawk superhero, and she’s joined by Kiawentiio as Wáhta, Jeremy White as Atahraks, Gabriel Romero as Rodrigo Alphonso Gonzolo, and the legendary Benedict Cumberbatch as Doctor Strange Supreme.

Diving into “What If…? Season 2”

Now that we know who Kahhori is, let’s talk about the series that introduced her – “What If…? Season 2.” This American animated series on Disney+ explores alternate timelines within the MCU. Launched on December 22, 2023, as part of MCU’s Phase Five, the season consists of six episodes released daily until December 30. Yeah, they’re keeping us on the edge of our seats!

Episode Guide – What If…? Season 2

For those of you keeping track, here’s a quick guide to the episodes in Season 2:

What If… Nebula Joined the Nova Corps? (Directed by Stephan Franck)

What If… Peter Quill Attacked Earth’s Mightiest Heroes? (Directed by Bryan Andrews)

What If… Happy Hogan Saved Christmas? (Directed by Bryan Andrews)

What If… Iron Man Crashed into the Grandmaster? (Directed by Bryan Andrews)

What If… Captain Carter Fought the Hydra Stomper? (Directed by Bryan Andrews)

What If… Kahhori Reshaped the World? (Directed by Bryan Andrews)

What If… Hela Found the Ten Rings? (Directed by Bryan Andrews)

What If… the Avengers Assembled in 1602? (Directed by Bryan Andrews)

What If… Strange Supreme Intervened? (Directed by Bryan Andrews)

“What If…? Season 2” Where to Watch?

“What If…? Season 2” is available for streaming on Disney Plus, with the latest episode, Episode 6, currently accessible. New episodes are being released daily until December 30. The series, part of Marvel Studios’ What If…? anthology, explores alternate storylines within the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
