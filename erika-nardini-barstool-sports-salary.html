What Does Erika Nardini Do For Barstool Sports?

When people look at what Erika Nardini has done for Barstool Sports, it is difficult to overstate how impressive her time as the company’s CEO has been. According to reports, under Erika Nardini’s stewardship, Barstool Sports went from a company with 12 employees to one where more than 600 people work. On top of that, when Nardini began working for Barstool Sports, it was a small company. According to celebritynetworth.com, Barstool Sports became so successful under Nardini’s control that Penn National Gaming paid $450 million for 36% of the company.

As Barstool Sports CEO, there are some parts of Erika Nardini’s job that are obvious to everyone. For example, Nardini often promotes the company during interviews and she has to respond to Dave Portnoy’s latest scandals. A few years ago, Erika Nardini was interviewed by Kara Goldin. During that interview, Nardini was asked what the most surprising part of being Barstool Sports’ CEO has been for her. In her response, Nardini revealed a lot about what her job for the company entails behind the scenes.

“I just was surprised about how much we could create and how much we could do simultaneously.” While Nardini’s response to that question revealed she enjoys being creative, later in the interview she makes it pretty clear that one of the biggest parts of her job is fixing everyone’s problems.

“It can be a grind. Everybody in your company has a problem at any given minute about multiple things, and having the stamina to work through that I think is important. I also think there’s a lot of … I like to be really involved. I think there’s CEOs who want to be distanced from their company, or they only talk to their management team, or stuff like that. I don’t really like that; I want to be really close to the people doing the most interesting things here.”

Based on what Erika Nardini said in the aforementioned interview, it seems clear her job principally consists of two things. First off, Nardini has the freedom to be creative in coming up with new opportunities for Barstool Sports which explains how she has managed to make the company so much more successful. On top of that, Nardini spends her days fixing the problems that her employees deal with.

While it is fascinating to know that figure, that doesn’t provide an answer to the question, how much is Nardini’s current Barstool Sports salary.

According to several different websites, Erika Nardini’s current annual salary is more than $600,000. For example, the website comparably.com which reports on the money that executives for various companies make says that Barstool Sports' highest-paid employee makes $600,000 a year. Considering that Nardini is Barstool Sports CEO and she has shepherded the company to great success, it is all but certain she gets paid more than any of the company’s other employees. On top of that, glamourpath.com also reports that Nardini gets paid $600,000 a year. Of course, it is also worth noting that bonuses likely would make that figure rise substantially.
