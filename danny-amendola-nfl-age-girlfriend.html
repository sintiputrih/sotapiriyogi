Well the NFL career of Danny Amendola is already on the peak. After playing a season for the Miami Dolphins he recently signed a contract with the Detroit Lions and is likely to make impressive earnings so far.

Danny Amendola is an American football wide receiver, who currently plays for the Detroit Lions of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football for Texas Tech Red Raiders football, a college football team of the Texas Tech University.

Danny Amendola Bio; Parents, Education, Height, Weight

Daniel James Amendola was born on 2nd November 1985 to parents Willie Amedola and Rose Amedola. He grew up along with an elder brother Matt in The Woodlands, Texas.

Amendola pursued his early education at The Woodlands High School. Afterward, he went to Texas Tech University and majored in Communication Studies. He stands at a height of 5 feet 11 inches (1.80 meters) and his listed weight is 190 lbs (86 kg).

His Career; NFL, Stats

Danny played college football for Texas Tech from 2004 to 2007. There he had 204 receptions for 2,246 yards and 15 touchdowns, and returned 116 punts for 1,283 yards and one touchdown, giving him an average of 11.06 yards per return.

Danny Amendola’s NFL career stats (Picture: Pro Football Reference)

In April 2008, he signed as an undrafted free agent with the NFL side Dallas Cowboys. After his contract expired at the Cowboys, Amedola moved to the Philadelphia Eagles in January 2009. He then joined the St. Louis Rams and began his first NFL action in 2009. He also played for NFL teams such as New England Patriots (2013-2017) and Miami Dolphins (2018). Recently, Danny joined the Detroit Lions on 11 March 2019.

Contract & Salary

Danny signed a 1 year contract with the Detroit Lions, worth $4,500,000 in March 2019. It includes $4,250,000 guaranteed, and an average annual salary of $4,500,000.
