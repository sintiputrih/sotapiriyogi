John Deere X738 Specs, Price &amp; Review 2022

April 2024 · 7 minute read

John Deere X738 Specs: The John Deere X738 lawn and garden tractor is a four-wheel drive (4WD) model from the John DeereX-series. John Deere Horicon Works, Horicon, Wisconsin, USA, has produced the John DeereX738 garden tractor since 2013.

John Deere X738 lawn mower used the Kawasaki FD750D EFI motor. Kawasaki FD750D EFI is a 0.75 l (45.46 cu-in) V-twin 90deg internal combustion small gasoline engine with a horizontal shaft.

The FD711D DFI features OHV (overhead valve design), cast-iron cylinder liners, a multi-port digital fuel injector; a high-pressure transistorized petrol pump; flywheel magneto-transistor ignition system.

The cylinder bore and stroke are 78.0mm (3.07in) and 78.0mm (3.07in), respectively. The compression ratio rating of the cylinder is 8.5 to 1. Kawasaki FD750D EFI produced 25.5 HP (19.0 kW), at 3,600 rpm.

John Deere’s X738 tractor comes with the Tuff-Torq K90 shaft-driven, two-pedal foot-controlled hydrostatic transmission (infinite forward/reverse), internal wet disk brakes, and hydrostatic power steering.

The tractor had an open operator station, a 19.7 L (5.2 US gallon) fuel tank, and an internal wet disc brake system.

How many horsepower is a John Deere X738?

The 25.5-hp (19 kW) liquid-cooled V2-twin engine has a 45.5-in. (745-cc) displacement and provides plenty of power to ensure excellent performance in all operating conditions. It is hinged so that the machine can be opened.

Where is the serial number on a John Deere X738?
