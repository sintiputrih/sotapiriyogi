What is Robbie Robertson’s Net Worth and Salary?

Robbie Robertson’s net worth is estimated to be around $10 million in 2021. It’s important to note that his fortune comes from his extensive career as a musician, songwriter, and producer.

As part of The Band, Robertson earned a large portion of his wealth. The band was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1994 and the Canadian Music Hall of Fame in 1989. They produced numerous hit songs, which helped to generate significant revenue.

Robertson has also worked outside of The Band, producing and songwriting for various artists, which has helped him earn a considerable amount of money. He has worked with artists such as Bob Dylan, Neil Diamond, and Eric Clapton.

Early Life and Education

Robbie Robertson was born on July 5, 1941, in Toronto, Canada. He was the son of a Jewish father and Mohawk mother. Robertson grew up on the reservation, where he was exposed to traditional Native American music that would eventually influence his work as a musician.

Robertson left school at a young age to pursue music full-time. He joined his first band, The Hawks, at the age of sixteen, and this marked the beginning of his long and illustrious career in music.

Career

Robertson’s most significant achievements came as the guitarist and primary songwriter for The Band. They started as a backup group for rockabilly singer Ronnie Hawkins, and later on, they would back up Bob Dylan during his American “gone electric” tour. Eventually, The Band produced their own music that would go on to become classic rock staples.

After the breakup of The Band, Robertson released a solo album in 1987, which received widespread acclaim. He has also worked on film soundtracks and earned an Academy Award nomination for his work on The Color of Money soundtrack.

Aside from his performing career, Robertson has also produced and written songs for various artists. His songwriting style has influenced countless musicians and has had a significant impact on the music industry.

Personal Life

Robertson has been married twice. His first marriage was to Dominique Bourgeois, who passed away in 2012. He has two children with Bourgeois. He is currently married to his longtime girlfriend, Priscilla Coolidge.

Throughout his career, Robertson has also been involved in various philanthropic endeavors. He has been a part of the Playing for Change Foundation, which uses music as a tool to create positive change in the world.

Real Estate

Robertson has owned several properties throughout his life. He owned a house in Woodstock, New York until 2018, which was sold for $1.4 million. He currently owns a home in Beverly Hills, California, which is estimated to be worth around $5 million.

Legacy

Robertson’s legacy in the music industry is significant. His work with The Band and his collaborations with other artists have helped to shape the sound of rock and roll over the past several decades. It’s not uncommon to see young musicians citing his songwriting style and guitar work as influences on their own work.
