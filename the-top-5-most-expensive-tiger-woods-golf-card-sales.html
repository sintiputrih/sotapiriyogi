The Unprecedented Sales


2001 Tiger Woods SP Authentic Golf Gold Auto #45 Rookie Card /100

In April 2021, Goldin Auctions sold a PSA 10 of this card for $369,000 setting an all-time high for any Tiger Woods Card. This card got beloved round internet, all based on the rare cause of how tough this is to find /100 PSA 10 Gem Mint graded pristine rookie card.

1998 Tiger Woods Champions of Golf Masters Collection

Sold on eBay for $64,300 on 2/19 honoring Tiger Woods' brilliant play at the 1997 Masters What makes it even more alluring is that only two cards are known to have ever received a PSA 10 grade.


Sports Illustrated (SI) For Kids 1996 Tiger Woods #536

This card also sold in April of 2021 where a PSA-10 graded one fetched $41,500. This card is special because it captures Tiger's first year as a pro and is named the PGA Rookie of the Year.


2001 Upper Deck Tiger Woods (#1)


A BGS 10 Black of this card was sold on eBay for $25k in February 2021. This card is widely considered to be Tiger's first mainstream rookie which only helps it appeal.
