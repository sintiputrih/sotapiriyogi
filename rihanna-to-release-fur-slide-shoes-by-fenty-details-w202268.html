Rihanna to Release Fur Slide Shoes by Fenty: Details

April 2024 · 1 minute read

The girl’s been hard at work!

On April 11, Puma announced that Rihanna‘s newest shoe from the Spring/Summer 2016 Fenty Puma by Rihanna collection will be released on Friday, April 22.

The Fur Slide, the third from her collection, is a remake of the “timeless PUMA Leadcat performance silhouette worn by football players off the pitch,” and “great to rock with socks or without,” a Puma press release reads, “for the poolside or hanging around the city.” The slide-on shoe, which is made of cruelty-free fur, has a satin foam backing. The brand’s signature cat is emblazoned on the shoe’s top, and FENTY PUMA by Rihanna is stamped on the heel and footbed. The $80 slide will come in white, black and shell.
