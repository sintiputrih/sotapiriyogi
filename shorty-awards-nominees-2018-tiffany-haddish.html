Believe it or not, the Shorty Awards are celebrating their 10th anniversary in 2018! The ceremony is set to take place on April 15 in New York City, and the nominees were just announced on January 16. HollywoodLife has the full list for you below, which includes breakout star Tiffany Haddish, rapper Cardi B and so many more familiar names. These awards are voted for by you, the fans, so once you’ve checked out the nominees click HERE to start voting!

The Shortys are an annual awards show that recognize people, organizations and brands who excel in producing short-form content on social media. This includes Twitter, Facebook, YouTube, Snapchat, Instagram and the rest of the internet. In the past, familiar names like Chrissy Teigen, Shay Mitchell and BTS have been honored by the Shorty Awards! Alright, now onto what you came here for — the nominees!
