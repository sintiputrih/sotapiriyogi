Vanessa Bryant, the wife of late basketball star Kobe Bryant, posted adorable photos of her daughters on Instagram. The kids were seen sharing sweet moments with their Auntie Kristin and Uncle P.

In recent photos on her Instagram feed, Vanessa Bryant, wife of the late basketball player Kobe Bryant, showed her beautiful daughters posing with their Auntie Kristin and Uncle P.

In the first photo, her daughter Bianka was seen smiling widely into the camera alongside Rob Pelinka and his wife, Kristin. Pelinka is the Vice President of Basketball Operations and general manager of the Los Angeles Lakers.

Vanessa Bryant and Kobe Bryant at the 2018 Vanity Fair Oscar Party at Wallis Annenberg Center for the Performing Arts on March 4, 2018 | Photo: Getty Images

Bianka was clad in a jean shirt and a patterned green wool jacket with her curly hair falling down the sides of her face as she grinned. She was perched in Kristin's lap while Pelinka had his hands resting on both their shoulders as they gazed into the camera.

In the second photo, Bianka and her sister Capri sat on either side of Pelinka who was dressed in a dark blue "Black Lives Matter" shirt and a Lakers face cap. Capri was dressed in an adorable striped dress and had her curly hair styled to the side.

Vanessa Laine Bryant at the 2018 Baby2Baby Gala on November 10, 2018 | Photo: Getty Images

Though she keeps a relatively private life, Vanessa has, over the months, kept her fans up to date with her life through several heartwarming snaps on Instagram.

On Tuesday, she shared a touching photo of her new leg tattoo. Just above her ankle, the 38-year-old intricately inked the names Kobe, Vanessa, Natalia, Gianna, Bianka, and Capri.

Vanessa also posted a throwback photo of Kobe and Gianna as a tribute for the National Father-Daughter day.

Photo of Vanessa Bryant's daughter, Bianca, with Auntie Kristin and Uncle P. | Photo: Instagram/vanessabryant

The mother of four also shared a mirror selfie where she was seen in the tattoo parlor. She was dressed in all black, except for her grey nose mask and heels. She then stuck out her lovely leg in a bid to show off the new tattoo.

The tattoo was said to be in honor of the memory of her family of six. The Bryants suffered a massive tragedy when Kobe, his 13-year-old daughter Gianna, and many others were killed in a helicopter crash in January.

Photo of Vanessa Bryant's daughters, Bianca, and her sister Capri, with Uncle P. | Photo: Instagram/vanessabryant

Eight months after the incident, Vanessa shared a touching picture of 3-year-old Bianka holding a black and white framed photo of the late basketball star. The picture struck a raw chord in the hearts of her fans and followers.

On Sunday, Vanessa also posted a throwback photo of Kobe and Gianna as a tribute for the National Father-Daughter day. The picture also celebrated the Lakers' 106-93 championship win against the Miami Heat.
