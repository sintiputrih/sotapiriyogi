Kadeem Hardison &amp; Chant Moore's Daughter Sophia Poses in a Rainbow-Printed Croptop in New Photos

October 2024 · 3 minute read

Kadeem Hardison and his ex-wife Chanté Moore's daughter Sophia showed off her toned physique in a rainbow-printed crop-top and black pants, looking all grown up.

Sophia Hardison served her Instagram fans with some brand new snaps of herself and it is obvious that she is growing up to be a fine woman.

Intricate tattoos on her upper chest were visible beneath her top. She also showed off the ink on the side of her palm and another small one was visible on her shoulder, too.

KADEEM AND CHANTÉ'S PAST

Sophia is the only daughter born out of her parents Kadeem Hardison and Chanté Moore's marriage. The couple married on November 17, 1997, a year after she was born.

Before getting married, Hardison and Moore had been together for nearly seven years. They were pretty private about their relationship all along.

Kadeem Hardison and singer Chante Moore attend the screening of Beverly Hills Cop 3 on May 22, 1994, in California. | Source: Getty Images

After almost three years of marriage, the couple decided to call it quits and Hardison filed for a divorce, seeking joint custody of their daughter.

Before the "A Different World" actor met Moore, he said that actress Cree Summer was his one true love but the way he loved Moore was something else. Today, he and Moore get along just fine even though they are not together.

STILL A FAMILY

After the divorce, Hardison and Moore maintained a good relationship as they co-parented their daughter. In fact, the R&B singer calls her ex-husband a friend and she also revealed in an interview with "Madame Noire" once that he is pleasant to be around.

"He's hilarious. He makes me bend over and hold my stomach in laughter. He's so naturally funny! I get him and he gets me. He appreciated me for who I was."

According to Moore, she and Hardison have grown together and she believes that is what friendship is all about. She even said that what they had was honest love.

Moore shared that she is going to be there for her ex no matter what. She also shared how every time she sees him, she is like, "there is my baby," but she does not want to be his girl.

Kadeem Hardison attends the premiere of OWN's "Love Is" on June 11, 2018 in Los Angeles, California. | Source: Getty Images

Throughout their union, the couple loved and respected one another and even after they parted ways, the respect was still there. And the best part is, they still consider each other family.

Hardison has not married another woman since his divorce with Moore. Meanwhile, the singer married Kenny Lattimore in 2002 but it did not last long. They share a son named Kenny Jr.
