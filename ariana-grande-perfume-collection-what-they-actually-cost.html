Here's What Ariana Grande Has Said About Her Perfume Collection And What They Actually Cost

February 2024 · 6 minute read

Ariana Grande Is a successful singer-songwriter and actress. She began in the music industry when she was only 15 and debuted her first album "yours truly" the 1950s-influenced pop and R&B which topped the US Billboard 200 in 2013. Grande became well-known for her roles as Cat Valentine in the Nickelodeon television series "Victorious" and "Sam & Cat". As one of the most iconic pop stars of this generation, Grande has a large following on social media and in 2019, she became the most-followed woman on Instagram. Grande has built a legacy of success extending beyond her music and has an estimated net worth of $240 million. It's no surprise Grande ventured into the fragrance industry and has released so far thirteen perfumes including limited editions.

Ariana Grande has won the FiFi Award several times and In 2022, one of her perfumes was the best-selling item at Ulta Beauty, one bottle was sold every eleven minutes. Grande's perfume line is the most searched celebrity product, with 4.4 million searches across Google and social media platforms. Ariana Grande has exceeded $1 billion in retail sales from her perfume collection. Here's what she has to say about her perfume collection and how much they cost.

7 What Ariana Grande Has Said About Her Perfume, Cloud

Ariana Grande has been nothing but complimentary about her Cloud perfume. She told Allure That it smells like "heaven in a bottle." She said that the scent was inspired by "the power of clouds, a reminder that the sky is the limit and anything is possible." It's no surprise that Cloud is the outlier among all her other perfumes.

The Cloud collection was unveiled in 2018, and its prominent scent is a whipped touch of coconut with vanilla orchids, blended with a bit of lavender blossom, juicy pear, and bergamot. The cloud is shaped like a cloud in an adorable blue and white color, with a rainbow or sun-like thing peeking out from behind it and capped by another cloud.

Cloud was officially described as an "uplifting scent that imbues a thoughtful artistic expression of positivity and happiness." Some of her fans said it was simply 'super cute'. Cloud by Ariana Grande retails for $65.

6 What Ariana Grande Had To Say About Her Perfume, Ari

The next scent in Ariana Grande's collection is her Ari perfume. Ari was unveiled in September 2015. In fact, in her own words, Grande has described the Ari by Ariana Grande scent as "so fun and true to me." "The scent is sweet yet sexy, with some surprises underneath." She describes it as "an all-encompassing, sultry, warm aroma."

She told Glamour that Ari was inspired by the strength of femininity and the power of being unapologetically yourself. With its blush pink hues and classic round shape, as well as the texture of a gem, the bottle exudes pure elegance, not to mention the extra design feature: a detachable plush white puff.

The box also has the singer's signature on it. Its scent is said to have mixtures ranging from raspberry, pear, and grapefruit as top notes to vanilla orchid, rose, and lily-of-the-valley as middle notes and marshmallow, musk, and wood as base notes. 3.4 oz of Ari by Ariana Grande retails for $65.

5 What Ariana Grande Said About Her 'Moonlight', Perfume

Another scent in Ariana Grande's collection is called Moonlight. Moonlight, a floral fruity perfume for women, is said to have a more romantic feel than other scents and was launched in 2017 as part of a campaign to create an "illuminating" connection between Grande and her fans.

Ariana referred to it as "a sexy, modern, musky scent." She told people that she was "inspired by the idea of embracing the night and all of its possibilities." The bottle looks like a purple disco ball that glimmers in the light, and just like Ari, it has a large poofy ball of soft purple fluff. Its smell is described as black currant, juicy plum, fluffy marshmallow, creamy sandalwood, black amber, and sensual vanilla. Ariana Grande's Moonlight costs $65 at retail.

4 What Ariana Grande Said About Her 'Thank U, Next' Perfume

In August 2019, Ariana Grande announced the arrival of her new perfume Thank U, Next. “I was inspired to design a bottle that represented the message of my song 'thank u, next' the emergence of the perfume from the broken heart represents moving forward from a challenging chapter.” This pertained to her breakup with her ex. This perfume costs $65 for 3.4 oz.

3 What Ariana Grande Had To Say About Her Perfume, God Is A Woman

Ariana Grande's third single off of her Grammy award-winning album "Sweetener", "God is a woman" was released in 2018 and gave way to the singer debuting her ninth perfume, aptly named after the hit song.

God is a woman has a top note of pear and ambrette, also it has a fruity note of vanilla and musk, and is described as her first ever clean fragrance composed of 91% naturally derived clean ingredients.

Ariana said in a statement, "I am beyond thrilled to celebrate the release of our first ever clean fragrance," she also said, "we have, of course, been cruelty-free but taking this next step moving into clean, with responsibly sourced materials and vegan ingredients, is something we are all very proud of. I adore this fragrance and hope it makes everyone wearing it feel beautiful and empowered." God is a woman 3.4 oz retails for $65 on ulta.com.

2 What Ariana Grande Has Said About Her Perfume, R.E.M

R.E.M perfume was launched in 2020 and is also the fourth track on Ariana Grande's album. She described her song as "a relationship that blurs the lines between the dream world and reality." which is why people were not surprised at the release of the perfume.

R.E.M. is said to be about soaring through the cosmos and is described as "an intergalactic dream of femininity and power." At first glance, the galaxy-inspired concept and dreamy name may appear odd, but Ariana said this of the perfume, "In my dreams, I'm always the version of myself I was in break free tbh." meaning she dreams in space and one can only imagine what R.E.M smells like.

R.E.M. bottle sits in the middle of some shooting crystals. R.E.M retails for $65 3.4oz on Ulta.com.

1 What Ariana Grande Has Said About Her Perfume, Sweet Like Candy

Sweet Like Candy was inspired by her third album. The sweet scent was created to represent how she feels whenever she listens to her favorite songs. Ariana had this to say about sweet like candy, "Launching Ari was a dream, and just like Ari, I wanted this to be something my fans would love, so the vibe is sweet, fun, and sexy." Sweet like candy was the star of its collection. It featured notes of cotton candy, marshmallows, and vanilla. 3.4 oz is sold for $65 on ulta.com.

Sweet like Candy has a Limited Edition as well. Ariana released a limited edition of her Sweet Like Candy fragrance in 2017. This scent was slightly different from the Ari by Ariana collection, with notes of raspberry, vanilla, and musk. Sweet Like Candy Limited Edition perfume 3.4 oz is also available on ulta.com for $65 USD.
