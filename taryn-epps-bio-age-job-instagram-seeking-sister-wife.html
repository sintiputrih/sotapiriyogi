Meet Seeking Sister Wife's Taryn Epps! Her Bio, Age, Job, Instagram

July 2024 · 5 minute read

TLC’s Seeking Sister Wife documents the life of polygamist families who are in various phases of seeking, dating, or transitioning a new sister wife into their lives. The 4th season of the popular TLC returned in June 2022 after a long hiatus; it features three new polygamist families alongside two returning couples. The Epps family, which consists of Marcus, India, and Taryn, is one of the three new polygamist families starring in Seeking Sister Wife Season 4.

Here, in this article, we will talk about Taryn and her relationship with other Epps members. You will also find about Taryn’s job and age-related info here!

Taryn Epps On Seeking Sister Wife

As of this writing, Marcus is legally married to Taryn and he is only engaged to India. While he will marry India anytime soon, the family doesn’t want to stop there and are in search of a third wife who will make their family whole.

Of course, if the show is about a polygamist family, a whole lotta drama is expected. Interestingly enough, viewers were introduced to some relationship drama that’s going on in the Epps family in the trailer itself. In the trailer, viewers saw that the triad has agreed on a curfew for spending time out of the house. However, when Marcus ignores the curfew time while going out on a date with another woman, India gets upset.

About their kids, we were not sure if he has any children with his wife Taryn Epps, or even his fiancee India. However, if Marcus’s Instagram is anything to go by, he does seem to have several kids, most probably from his previous relationships.

Seeking Sister Wife wished his firstborn, Tahj Epps, the happiest 20th birthday through a sweet Instagram post on March 27, 2022. In the caption, he wrote, “HAPPY BIRTHDAY TO MY FIRST BORN SON. I AM SO PROUD OF YOU SOLDIER!… WE LOVE YOU TO ALASKA AND BACK, BABY BOY!!! LOL.”

According to Tahj’s Instagram, he is already married. Reportedly, Tahj is the Founder of God’s Property Youth Ministries and currently works in US Army.

Marcus’s other son turned 16 years old on March 15, 2022. Overall, Marcus seems to love all his kids equally.

  • Marcus Epps Controvsersy

The Epps family may seem like a happy, positive family from the outside, but the Cleveland 19 News report from early 2021 tells a whole other story.

According to the report, Marcus was allegedly connected to an “illegal establishment,” which operated as a “known after-hours club.” In late 2020, it was reported that an unidentified woman was shot and killed at the club in the same establishment. A video that surfaced after the incident showed Marcus at the crime scene carrying another injured person out.

In the summer of 2021, police executed a search warrant of Marcus’s home because several of his duplexes were flagged for having multiple housing violations. Marcus noted the situation as “a political prosecution by the city of Euclid,” and seemed to deny any involvement in the 2020 shooting.

Taryn Epps Age

Taryn Epps’ age was 37 as of this writing. Her mom wished her a happy 35th birthday back in 2020.

What Is Taryn Epps Maiden Name?

Taryn Epps’ maiden name is Lindsey.

Taryn Epps Job

As of this writing, we knew nothing about Taryn Epps’ job. Nor did we know what other members of the Epps family do to make money.

Taryn Epps Height

Seeking Sister Wife star Taryn Epps stands at an average height of 5 feet 8 inches.

Is Taryn Epps On Instagram?

Although Taryn Epps does not have her own Instagram, updates related to her life and the other members of The Epps Family can be found on their family IG handle @sisme_andhe. The family IG handle had over 4.3 thousand followers as of this writing. The polygamist Epps family also has their own TikTok account.

Taryn also has a personal Facebook account. However, it seems like she is not active over there as her last Facebook post came on October 21, 2018.

Related FAQs

  • When Is Taryn Epps Birthday?

Taryn celebrates her birthday every June 2nd. Her man, Marcus, wished her a happy 37th birthday by posting a sweet video on Instagram.

  • Where Is Taryn Epps From?

Taryn Epps is originally from Aliquippa, Pennsylvania. Although her Facebook says that her current state of residence is in Cleveland, Ohio, the Epps family is residing in Miami, Florida in the show Seeking Sister Wife.

  • What Do We Know About Taryn Epps Family?

Talking about Taryn Epps’s family, she is very close to her mother, Teri Lindsey. On September 26, 2020, she wished Teri and her other daughter on the occasion of Daughters Day.

As much as we know, Taryn has at least two siblings. Her sister’s name is Teshanti Riggins. Meanwhile, Taryn’s brother, Dwight Lindsey works at Aliquippa School District and is a Special Education Teacher at PA Cyber Charter School according to his Facebook. He was a former Paraeducator at Holy Family Institution. Dwight was 40 years old as of June 2022.

Some other members of Taryn’s family are also Kandice, Cameron, and Andre Lindsey.
