Doja Cat andMonica Bellucci ooze elegance as they leave luxe hotel following Dolce &amp; Gabbana event

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Doja Cat and Monica Bellucci cut extremely elegant figures on Monday as they left the Lido Excelsior Hotel in Venice following the Dolce & Gabbana event.   

Doja Cat, 25, sported a classy look consisting of a baby blue flowery dress that she completed with red heels – and the same stunning shade of lipstick.  

Meanwhile, Monica, 56, donned a sophisticated black ensemble and put safety first by wearing a facemask. 

Sophisicated: Doja Cat, 25 (above) and Monica Bellucci, 56, oozed elegance as they left the Lido Excelsior Hotel on Monday following Dolce & Gabbana event in Venice

Doja, real name Amala Ratna Zandile Dlamini, looked incredibly chic as she posed by the water’s edge, sporting black cat-eyed sunglasses.

The rapper carried a colourful Dolce crotchet clutch, the red element coordinating with her shoes and lipstick. 

She coiffed her raven bob effortlessly so not a single hair was out of place.

Monica, who is renowned for being an actress and model, wore a stunning blouse that had flowy detailing.

She teamed this with black flared jeans that were completed by a subtle peek of her heels.

The Italian beauty was spotted boarding a boat as she left the luxe Venetian hotel.

Their stunning appearances follow Dolce & Gabbana’s non-fungible token (NFT) collection launch which took place on Saturday.  

The collection called Collezione Genesi (Genesis Collection), which will debut on the luxury marketplace UNXD at a later date, is said to ‘bridge the physical with the metaphysical’ and featured items personally designed by co-founders Domenico Dolce and Stefano Gabbana themselves.

The concept was inspired by a dream from one of the designers, called Dress from a Dream, and will focus on the rich artistic history and artistic traditions of the famous floating city. 

The new NFT range will be shown at Dolce & Gabbana’s Alta Moda couture show on Saturday in Venice, with an official live auction kicking off on the 6th September.
