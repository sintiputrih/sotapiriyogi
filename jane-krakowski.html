Name:Jane Krakowski

Hometown:Parsippany-Troy Hills, NJ

Birthday:October 11, 1968

About

Jane Krakowski is a Screen Actors Guild and Tony Award-winning actress. Born October 11, 1968, the New Jersey native gained notoriety thanks to roles in the soap opera Search for Tomorrow, the FOX series Ally McBeal and the Emmy Award-winning NBC comedy 30 Rock.

Television credits aside, Krawkowski has also appeared in films such as National Lampoon’s Vacation, Fatal Attraction and Alfie. In 1996, she starred alongside Sarah Jessica Parker in the Broadway revival of Once Upon a Mattress. She won a Tony Award in 2003 for her role in Nine.

Prior to marrying British-born clothing designer Robert Godley in January 2010, Krakowski dated English documentarian Marc Singer and actor Julian Ovenden. She and Godley welcomed son Bennett on April 13, 2011.
