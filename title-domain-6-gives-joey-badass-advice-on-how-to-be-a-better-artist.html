Joey Badass Asks Twitter For Advice, &amp; Is Told To Collab With Kanye West &amp; Kendrick Lamar

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Having just dropped his latest album, All-Amerikkkan Bada$$, Joey Bada$$ isn’t content to sit on his laurels. He’s been engaging with fans, asking on Twitter what he can do differently to improve.

He took to Twitter Monday (May 1) to ask: “Talk to me guys. What do I need to improve on to become a better artist? Assuming the music is all good, what outside of the music should I improve on?”

Talk to me guys. What do I need to improve on to become a better artist?

— BADMON (@joeyBADASS) May 1, 2017

Assuming the music is all good, what outside of the music should I improve on?

— BADMON (@joeyBADASS) May 1, 2017

In 16 hours, his (now-deleted) questions received more than 1,500 responses… which might lead someone to believe that Twitter’s collective answer was “a lot.”

AD LOADING...

Of course, a lot of the advice didn’t have much to do with changes that Joey could make to his own style or the way he promotes his music. One of the most popular responses was simply “Make a Kanye collab.” Even more popular was the suggestion that he make a song with Vince Staples, Chance the Rapper and Kendrick Lamar.

@joeyBADASS Make a kanye collab

— Rodneyy ? (@watch_myfeet) May 1, 2017

@joeyBADASS make a song with vince, chance & kendrick

— ㅤ (@V47UED) May 1, 2017

One sort of ironic tweet suggested that the key was… communication with fans, something Joey was already in the middle of doing.

@joeyBADASS communication with fans.

— نار (@izayatyler) May 1, 2017

There were also some heartwarming messages from fans who think that Joey is pretty much perfect as he is.

@joeyBADASS nothing. you already doing what you meant to do. and we fucc wit it.

— نار (@izayatyler) May 1, 2017

Either way, it looks like Joey was happy to get the advice, and he thanked all his fans in a separate tweet.

Appreciate y'all thoughts, ideas and opinions. Inspired ?

— BADMON (@joeyBADASS) May 1, 2017
