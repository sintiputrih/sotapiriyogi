Karan Brar is an American actor and social media personality. Karan Brar rose to fame from his roles in Diary of a Wimpy Kid, Jesse, and Dog Days. Similarly, he has established himself as a talented and versatile actor in the film industry.

Early Life

Karan Brar was born in 1999 and celebrated his birthday on January 18 every year. He was born in Redmond, Washington, USA. Similarly, the actor is 23 years old now. He belongs to American nationality and is of Indian origin. Moreover, he was born under the star sign Capricorn.

Career and Professional Life

Karan Brar is now an aspiring actor. He has won the hearts of many viewers with his natural and versatile acting skills. Likewise, he started acting at the age of 11. He appeared in the character of a wimpy kid Chirag Gupta in the comedy feature film ‘Diary of a Wimpy Kid’.

Furthermore, he also appeared in the same role in the third installment of the franchise. The third installment titled Diary of Wimpy Kid: Dog Days was released in August 2012. He had already won the hearts of several American households from his acting.
